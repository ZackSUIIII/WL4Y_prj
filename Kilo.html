Kilo

The Kilo Fruit is a Natural-type Blox Fruit. It costs $5,000, or $50, from the Blox Fruit Dealer. It also has a large chance to be obtained from the Blox Fruits Dealer Cousin. It was added in Update 15. This fruit has a 100% chance of being in stock, and a 13% chance of spawning in-game. Kilo is currently the cheapest fruit in the game and has replaced Bomb as the spotlight. It is the cheapest fruit you can get from the Blox Fruits Dealer. The money cost of Kilo in Update 17.2 was $5,000, or R$50 from the Blox Fruit Dealer.
